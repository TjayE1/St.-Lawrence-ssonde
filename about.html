About St. Lawrence S.S Ssonde

St. Lawrence S.S Ssonde, also surfaced publicly as St. Lawrence S.S.S - SONDE, NAMUGONGO, is a mixed day and boarding O and A-Level secondary school established in 1998 and part of the wider St. Lawrence Schools and Colleges network.

Our Mission & Values

Academic excellence, high discipline, moral standards and value-based personal formation are central to the school’s identity.

Campus & Facilities

Public snippets and campus photos point to well-kept grounds, classrooms, labs, and a strong emphasis on music, dance, drama and sports.
