About Us

Established in 1998, St. Lawrence S.S Ssonde serves the Sonde-Namugongo area and is widely described in public listings as a mixed day and boarding O and A-Level secondary school. It is part of the St. Lawrence Schools and Colleges family.

  • Founded: 1998
  • Core values: Academic excellence, high discipline, moral standards, value-based formation
  • Campus culture: Music, dance, drama, leavers' rock, prom and youth media features

Academics & Admissions

Public search results describe the school as offering both O-Level (UCE) and A-Level (UACE) streams in arts and sciences. The school also appears in public search snippets with a fee estimate that should be verified directly with the administration.

O-Level

UCE preparation, core sciences and arts, and theory-practical balance.

A-Level

UACE arts and sciences with career guidance and university pathway support.
