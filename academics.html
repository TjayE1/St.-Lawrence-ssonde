Academics

We offer a comprehensive O-Level and A-Level curriculum with a focus on sciences, arts, and commerce. Our teachers follow UNEB-aligned syllabi and provide exam preparation and mentorship.

Subject Combinations

  • O-Level: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, English, History
  • A-Level: Sciences (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), Arts (History, Divinity), Commerce (Economics, Accounting)

Support & Enrichment

Tutorials, revision camps, career guidance and university placement support are provided for senior students.
